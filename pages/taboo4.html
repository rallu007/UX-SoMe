How to play



Start from dividing into two teams. One team of boys and one of the girls. Why? Because we will check your knowledge from the terms connected with opposite sex.

You draw a word that your other teammates have to guess. Below, there are few other words that you can’t use during explaining the main one. Of course you also can’t use the top one, not the part of that. You have two minutes to guess as much as you can. If it’s going very bad and no one can guess, you can skip to the next card. When the time’s up you can see your result. And then it’s the next team. Easy. Enjoy!



Minimum players: 2 teams of at least 2
